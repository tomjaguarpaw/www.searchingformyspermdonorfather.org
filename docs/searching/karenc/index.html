Hello. My name is Karen. I am 29 years old, 5'4" with blue eyes and dark brown hair. My blood type is A-. I am as pale as a flourescent light bulb. I am anxious to meet my biological father or at least find out his medical history and ethnic background and locate my half brothers and sisters across the globe.

I was conceived at the University of Penn Hospital Fertility Clinic in Philadelphia PA. My mother received treatments on the mornings of June 17th, 18th, and 19th 1981.
